In this first part of the quiz, the rules are simple: You get to see 6 flags from nations all over the world and you have to guess the correct place. By hovering over (cursor devices) or touching the flag (mobile), you can see its answer.

~ Solve the riddle! ~

Unlike the questions, the rules of the second quiz section aren't difficult either. One is presented with a riddle and to check his or her answer, one can simply hover over (cursor devices) or or simply touch (Android mobile) the solution box or by holding touch on the box (iOS mobile).

What is the only country with a modern firearm on its flag? (Hint: It's an AK-47.)

~ Locate the flag! ~

The last part of the quiz is very interactive: You are presented with a Google Map in which you can find a flag by looking around. The task is then to guess the country of the location. You can check your answer by clicking on the map pointer in the top left corner and zooming out.
